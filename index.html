What's inside

A real exchange.
A real lesson.

JEX replicates the mechanics of a live stock market — price discovery, order flow, corporate actions, and portfolio management — in a safe classroom setting.

↑↓
Live price movement

Every buy pushes prices up, every sell pulls them down. Market impact is proportional to order size, just like the real thing.

IPO
IPO applications

Companies apply to list on JEX. The admin reviews and approves, then shares enter the market for students to trade.

div
Dividends

Listed companies can distribute dividends to shareholders at any time, paid instantly from company cash based on shareholding.

↺
Stock buybacks

Companies can buy their own shares back from the market, reducing supply and lifting the price. Requires company cash.

+%
Share dilution

Companies can issue new shares to raise capital. The dilution requires admin approval and proportionally adjusts the price.

↘
Short selling

Advanced traders can short stocks — borrowing shares to sell now and buy back cheaper later. Requires 1.5× collateral.

★
Watchlist & alerts

Students can track stocks they're watching, with live price updates and quick access to the trade panel from their portfolio.

№
Leaderboard

Real-time net worth rankings across all students. Cash, portfolio value, short P&L, and dividends all count toward the score.

⏱
Session timer

The admin controls the trading session — open, pause, or close it at any time, or run a countdown timer for timed trading rounds.

Getting started

How it works

For students
1
Register your account
Sign up with your name, email, and a password. Set a security question in case you ever need to reset it.
2
Wait for admin approval
Your teacher reviews and approves your account. You'll receive $10,000 in virtual cash to start trading.
3
Research the companies
Read company financials and earnings reports. Watch price charts and add stocks to your watchlist.
4
Trade and build your portfolio
Buy low, sell high — or short a stock you think will fall. Collect dividends and grow your net worth.
5
Climb the leaderboard
Your net worth is ranked live against every other student. Who finishes on top?
For companies
1
Register your company
Sign up with your company name, email, password, and a short description of your business.
2
Get approved by admin
Your teacher approves your company account. You'll start with company cash to fund operations.
3
Apply for your IPO
Submit an IPO application with your proposed share price and number of shares. Admin approves the listing.
4
Manage your company
Post earnings reports, pay dividends to your shareholders, run buybacks to support your share price, or dilute to raise more capital.
5
Watch your market cap grow
Every trade in your stock moves the price. Good earnings, smart capital management, and investor confidence drive your valuation.
Who's on JEX

Three roles,
one market.

Student
The Investor

You start with $10,000 and a blank portfolio. Every decision is yours — what to buy, when to sell, and how to manage risk.

  • Buy and sell listed shares
  • Short-sell with collateral
  • Earn dividends on holdings
  • Track stocks on your watchlist
  • Compete on the live leaderboard
Company
The Issuer

You run a company. Take it public, manage your share price, reward your shareholders, and make strategic capital decisions.

  • Apply for an IPO listing
  • Post public earnings reports
  • Pay dividends to shareholders
  • Execute share buybacks
  • Apply to issue new shares
Admin
The Regulator

You control the exchange. Approve accounts, review applications, manage trading sessions, and monitor every account in real time.

  • Approve student & company accounts
  • Review IPO & dilution applications
  • Open, pause, or close the session
  • Set countdown timers for rounds
  • Export live balances to Google Sheets
